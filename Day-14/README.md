Day 14 — AI Job Red Flag Detector
Overview

AI Job Red Flag Detector is a prompt-driven tool designed to help job seekers evaluate job opportunities before investing time in applications and interviews.

Instead of only focusing on whether you're qualified, this tool analyzes job descriptions and company information to uncover potential risks, misleading claims, toxic workplace indicators, and hiring concerns.

The goal is to help candidates make informed career decisions and avoid problematic employers.

Features
1. Unrealistic Requirements Detection

Identifies:

Excessive experience requirements
Long lists of required skills
Contradictory expectations
Junior roles demanding senior-level expertise
2. Toxic Workplace Signal Analysis

Flags phrases and patterns associated with:

Burnout culture
"Wear many hats" expectations
Hustle culture
"Rockstar" or "Ninja" hiring language
Poor work-life balance
3. Remote Job Authenticity Check

Detects:

Hidden office attendance requirements
Relocation expectations
Hybrid roles advertised as remote
Geographic restrictions
4. Hiring Risk Assessment

Analyzes:

Missing salary transparency
Vague responsibilities
Excessive qualification requirements
Suspicious hiring practices
5. Company Risk Evaluation

Reviews:

Stability concerns
Layoff indicators
Reputation issues
Growth sustainability signals
