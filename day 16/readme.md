Stock Fundamental Research
Overview

Stock Fundamental Research is an AI-powered equity research skill designed to analyze Indian and global listed companies using business fundamentals, financial statements, valuation metrics, capital allocation quality, competitive advantages, ownership patterns, and growth prospects.

The skill focuses on evidence-based analysis and investor education while strictly avoiding investment advice, target prices, or buy/sell/hold recommendations.

The skill:

✅ Analyzes business fundamentals

✅ Reviews financial statements

✅ Evaluates valuation

✅ Highlights risks

✅ Explains financial metrics

❌ Give Buy recommendations

❌ Give Sell recommendations

❌ Give Hold recommendations

❌ Provide target prices

❌ Predict future stock prices

❌ Offer personalized investment advice
